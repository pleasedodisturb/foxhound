## Key Tensions & Insights

**High Cooperativeness (78th) + High Self-Confidence (89th):** Can collaborate well but won't defer when confident in a direction. This exact combination caused friction at Wolt -- cooperative enough to be hired, confident enough to push for change that wasn't wanted.

**High Openness (83rd) + Low Conscientiousness (32nd):** The classic innovator-not-administrator profile. Generates creative solutions but may not document them in the "right" format or follow the "right" process. Needs environments that value output over process.

**High Extroversion (85th) + Average Motivation (57th):** Energized by people and ideas, but not uniformly driven -- selective about what to pour energy into. Will seem extremely motivated on interesting problems and disengaged on administrative busywork.

**Moderate Assertiveness (72nd) + Low Stress Tolerance (34th):** Will push for what's right, but the emotional cost of conflict is real. The Wolt farewell letter is a perfect artifact of this -- assertive and eloquent, but clearly written from a place of emotional impact.
