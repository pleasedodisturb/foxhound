# Strengths Summary: Unified Assessment View

Cross-referencing Gallup CliftonStrengths, Criteria Corp EPP, CCAT, and Workplace Insights.

## The Pattern Across Tests

Every assessment converges on the same profile: a **strategic, communicative, self-confident leader** who thrives on ideas, change, and autonomy -- and struggles with routine, rigid structure, and narrow roles.

## Workplace Insights (Criteria Corp, Aug 2024)

**Notable Traits:** Cooperative, Extroverted, Intellectually Curious, Self-Confident

**Comfort Zones:**
- Extremely comfortable: Open discussions, change in expectations/duties, taking the lead
- Somewhat comfortable: Having clear goals, being exposed to conflict
- Not comfortable: Having to work alone (extended), rigid rule enforcement, narrowly defined roles

**Key Development Areas:**
- Balance extroversion with listening -- let others be heard
- Don't over-engineer when simple solutions work
- Ensure confidence doesn't skip preparation
- Know when to change course vs. persist

## The Synthesis: Who Is Vitalik?

A **strategic communicator and activator** who sees the future, connects disparate ideas, and moves people to action. Strongest in roles that demand vision, persuasion, and adaptive problem-solving. Weakest in roles requiring rigid discipline, routine consistency, and detailed analytical tracking.

**Best environment:** High autonomy, evolving challenges, permission to experiment, AI-forward culture.
**Worst environment:** Rigid process, narrow mandate, political conformity, innovation theater.
